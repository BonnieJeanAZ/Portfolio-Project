Hi I am Bonnie Runyan

Frontend Developer based in Phoenix, Arizona

I am a native Arizonan born & raised. Now here I am raising a family of my own and I wouldn't have it any other way. I have always been a natural innovator and problem-solver. When you combine this with my entrepreneurial spirit, you can usually find me diving into a variety of new creative projects!

As a designer and frontend developer, my goal is always to provide innovative solutions through responsive design and scalable development for the best possible user experience. With a bachelor's degree in design, I have worked the last 12 years with a large e-commerce company which has provided me experience in graphic design, data analytics, and business & people management. After years of working closely with software engineers and project managers, I finally invested in learning coding for myself and don't plan to look back. The skills I have learned throughout my SheCodes Pro certification combined with my experience have me looking forward to new possibilities and opportunities.
